Gas Price

As the Ethereum network is a decentralised blockchain with no singular entity or authority overseeing its operation, a mechanism is put in place to avoid the network from being congested or spammed with transactions. This mechanism charges senders of transactions with a small fee called gas fee which is then used to reward miners who validate transactions on the network. This fee is in the form of Ether (ETH) which is also used on the Ethereum network to facilitate value transfers, mining payouts and smart contract executions.
